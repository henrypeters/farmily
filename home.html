What We Do

At Farmily, we offer range of services focused on sustainable agriculture like precision farming, education and community engagement through agritourism, educational programs, and community events. Farmily connects people with the agricultural process and promotes environmental stewardship. We also provide nature inspired accomodations and barbeque services to enhance farming experience, as well as gracing events with our services providing guests with oppurtunities to reconnect with nature and enjoy farm fresh cuisine both on the farm and event grounds.

About Us

Our Mission

Dedictated to nurturing healthy and productive livestock simultaneously providing value added farm products and services. Rooted in passion, sustainability and innovation. The farm's mission is to contribute positively to the agricultural landscape in Africa. With a focus on the revolutionary turn for African youths into agriculture, farmily is collaboratively shaping the agricultural space into a significant contributor to Africa's economy.

Our Vision

To develop a precision farming model which will inspire young African youths towards reverse migration from urban to rural areas, creating a pathway to prosperity.

Product

Our Products

Farmily practices sustainable and ethical precision livestock farming, raising diverse animials like pigs, cattle, poultry, sheep, and goats, ensuring proper care and nutrition. We offer various breeds to meet customer preferences. Farmily transforms meat and milk into value-added products like processed meats, diary items and specialty goods such as cheese and yougurt. These products adhere to strict quality standards and are marketed to both local & international consumers and businesses, highlighting their nutritional benefits and ethical production practices.

Creation

Ownership

Farmily germinated from the visionary partnership of Icent and Chiamaka Nwanua, driven by a shared commitment to revolutionize Africa's agricultural landscape. In this early stage, we have established a clear vision to create a transformative model that inspires the reverse migration of African youths from urban to rural areas. The farm's ethos, sets the foundation for a business plan dedicated to transform change in the agricultural sector.
