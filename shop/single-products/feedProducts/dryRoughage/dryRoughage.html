Roughages are usually ground before they are pelleted, size of the pellets range from 12/64" o 48/64" (4.8 mm to 19.1 mm). Pelleted roughages weigh about 40 lb/cft as compared to 5-6 lb/cft for long hay. Pelleting poor quality roughages will markedly increase the consumption of roughage.
